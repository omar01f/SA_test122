About Us

Akletna is a modern Egyptian-inspired restaurant created with a passion for serving fresh, authentic, and flavorful dishes. The idea began as a student project, but quickly turned into something bigger—a vision to bring traditional Egyptian taste into a well-designed digital experience.

Our goal is to create a dining atmosphere that blends comfort with tradition. From classic meals like koshary, molokheya, and mahshi, to modern twists on local favorites, we aim to deliver a memorable experience to every visitor.

At Akletna, we believe food should be simple, honest, and crafted with care. Our team works to ensure every dish reflects the warmth and hospitality of Egyptian culture.

Thank you for visiting Akletna. We hope our website reflects both our creativity and our dedication to bringing good food to everyone who loves Egyptian cuisine.
